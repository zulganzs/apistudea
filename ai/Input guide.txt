Input of the function is an array of the following:
Academic achievements (how good their grades are)
Non-Academic achievements (1 being none, 5 being an equivalent of an international competition winner)
Income (1 being very poor, 5 being very rich)
Motivation (1 being just trying out, 5 being desperate)
Interest: 
1 is stem(engineering, physics, math, etc)
2 is humanities(anthropology, psychology, literature, etc)
3 is business(management, accounting, economics, etc)
4 is art(liberal arts, sculpting, pottery, etc)
5 is others
